Hello! I'm a software engineer based in Boston, MA. I recently finished my Master's degree at ETH Zurich in Robotics, Systems, and Controls. Prior to that, I did my Bachelor's at the University of Texas at Dallas in Electrical Engineering. My passion is designing innovative products and features at the hardware-software interface. I am especially interested in human computer interfaces, the Internet of Things, and mobile robotics.


Some of my favorite projects were the automated hair clipper, the automated sleep support, the feedback mechanisms for real-walking virtual reality, and the harmonic radar system for tracking juvenile wood frogs. Read more about them on this portfolio site, and please don't hesitate to reach out for any additional details or questions.


In my freetime, I enjoy playing jazz piano, team sports (basketball, volleyball, Ultimate), and being in the outdoors (backpacking, rock climbing, skiing, mountain biking). I am an avid traveler and have lived in (+3 months) 10 different cities around the world.
