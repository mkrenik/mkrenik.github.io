Buskr - App for Street Musicians

HackJunction Helsinki Fall 2015

This was done as a hackathon project during HackJunction 2015 in Helsinki. Our team created a tool using the MixRadio API, Klarna API (payment), and an Estimote bluetooth beacon. It allows street musicians to get paid by people passing by that have the app installed. Additionally, it shows users where street musicians are located around the city in real time. Finally, it tracks the musicians that you have tipped and gives you music suggestions and purchase options for similar music. Our team won the MixRadio API prize for this project.
